A LITTLE ABOUT US..

Falvé is a menswear label based in Wellington, New Zealand. The label launched in March 2013 by husband and wife, Dan and Emma Johnstone.

Falvé produce limited runs of menswear garments and accessories. The garments and goods are designed to be simple with an emphasis on their construction and detail.

The patterns are handmade using traditional tailoring methods and all cloth is all hand cut.
From the Wellington workroom Falvé also produce the leather goods, shirt labels, swing tags and custom made packaging for the products.

ALL OUR GARMENTS ARE HANDCRAFTED IN WELLINGTON - RIGHT DOWN TO THE BUTTONS ,WHICH ARE INDIVISUALLY HAND STITCHED.


Our brand name Falvé, pronounced ‘Fal-vay’ was inspired by Emma’s maiden name – Falvey. If you would like more info please send us an email, we’d love to hear from you.
